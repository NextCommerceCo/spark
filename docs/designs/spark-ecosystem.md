## Vision

### 10x Check
Spark becomes the **storefront operating system for NEXT Commerce** — not just a theme, but a component platform. Web Components powered by GraphQL, AI-native customization via Claude Code, zero-friction developer tooling via ntk. Merchants get a premium default. Developers get a reference architecture. AI gets rails to build on.

### Platonic Ideal
A merchant opens their NEXT store for the first time. It already looks like it was designed by an agency — clean, sharp, products front and center. They tap "Customize," pick their brand color, choose a font, and watch their store transform in real-time. A developer opens the Spark repo, runs `make dev`, and is editing live in 10 seconds. Claude Code reads CLAUDE.md and can customize the entire storefront from a brief in under 5 minutes.
